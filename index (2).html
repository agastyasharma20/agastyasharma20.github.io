About the Doctor

A Healer You Can
Trust & Rely On

Dr. Anoop Sharma is a distinguished General Physician based in Wardhaman Nagar, Nagpur, with over 28 years of dedicated practice since 1998. Holding a Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine & Surgery (BHMS) along with a Certificate in Community Medicine & Public Health (CCMP), Dr. Sharma has earned the deep trust of thousands of families through his patient-first approach and compassionate care.

His philosophy is simple — treat the person, not just the disease. With a warm bedside manner and decades of clinical experience, he ensures every patient, from infants to the elderly, receives the time, attention, and care they truly deserve. Known for being genuinely accessible, affordable, and deeply caring about long-term wellness.
